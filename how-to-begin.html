Ready to Begin? Your First Steps

Follow these steps to redeem your credits and start your learning journey.

💳 Step 1: Redeem Your 9 Initial Credits

  • Open the redemption link in an Incognito/Private Window. Redemption Link
    Crucial: Make sure you are logged OUT of your Google Cloud Skills Boost (GCSB) account before opening the link, otherwise the credit redemption will fail.
  • When the prompt window appears, enter your specific voucher code.

    Your Voucher Code: (Check your selection email) 1q-genai-10091

  • After entering the code, finally login to your GCSB account. The SIGN IN option is usually available on the top right corner of the page.
  • Check your profile. You should see 9 credits successfully added to your account.
    Note: If you do not see the 9 credits, that means you didn't follow the initial steps (especially step 1) properly. Go back and repeat the process until the credits appear.

💰 Step 2: Unlock 400 Credits & Start Solving

  • Perform a sample tour lab or any lab you like. Once you complete that lab 100%, you will automatically receive 400 credits in your profile!
  • Complete the Level 3 Arcade Badge on priority to ensure participation in the main event.

    Arcade Link: Gen AI Arcade Game: Level 3

    Deadline for Level 3: 30 October 2025
  • After the Arcade, your main goal is to finish the 19 Challenge labs listed in the Skill Badges syllabus.
