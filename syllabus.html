How to Fast-track and Complete the Skill Badge

Step 1: Check Your Enrollment Email

• Look for an email about your course enrollment
• In this email, you'll find a link to your learning syllabus

Step 2: Access Your Syllabus

• Click the syllabus link in your email to open the list of skill badge labs
• Refer to the syllabus page for detailed course information

Syllabus Screenshot
Step 3: Select the Skill Badge Lab

• Choose your desired skill badge lab
• Example: 'Develop GenAI Apps with Gemini and Streamlit'

Step 4: Enter Challenge Lab

• Look for the blue banner labeled 'Ready for a challenge?'
• This banner contains your fast-track option

Challenge Lab Banner
Step 5: Start the Challenge

• Click the 'Start challenge' button
• Begin working on your lab tasks

Step 6: Complete Tasks

• Follow all instructions carefully
• Complete required tasks to achieve passing score

Step 7: Verify Completion

• Check your badge dashboard
• Verify your new skill badge appears

Badge Dashboard
Important Note

Do not start the challenge lab directly from the course listing. Always begin through the blue challenge banner to ensure proper tracking.
