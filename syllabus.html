🚀 How to Fastrack and Complete the Skill Badge

Bypass the full-length lab by directly attempting the Challenge Lab.

Step 1: Check Your Enrollment Email

Look for an email about your course enrollment. In this email, you'll find a link to your **learning syllabus**.

Step 2: Access Your Syllabus and View Badge Paths

Click the syllabus link in your email to open the list of skill badge labs. The page will look similar to this:

Step 3: Select the Skill Badge Lab

Click on the specific skill badge lab you want to complete (e.g., **'Develop GenAI Apps with Gemini and Streamlit'**).

Step 4: Enter the Challenge Lab Dashboard

After clicking the lab, you'll be redirected to its dashboard. Look for a prominent blue banner: **'Ready for a challenge? Fast-track your way to a new badge.'**

Step 5: Start the Challenge Lab

Click the **Start challenge** button on the blue banner to begin your lab.

Step 6: Complete the Challenge Lab

Work through the lab tasks, following all instructions. Finish the lab successfully with the required score or tasks.

Step 7: Check Your Dashboard

Once you've completed the challenge lab, go to your badge dashboard. The earned badge will show up in your Google Cloud Skill Boost dashboard.

⚠ **IMPORTANT:** Do not start the challenge lab directly from the course listing. Make sure to start it via the **blue banner** inside the Skill Badge Lab dashboard!
